03 · FLIP shared element expand

First, Last, Invert, Play. The card is measured in place, repositioned as a fixed modal, then the inverse transform is animated away. One element, no clones.

Specimen 03

Press to expand

This exact element becomes the modal. The readout shows the inverted transform the animation starts from.

In the FLIP technique the layout change happens instantly, then a transform makes the element appear to travel from its old position. Because only transform animates, the browser keeps it on the compositor thread and the motion stays smooth even on weak devices. Press Escape or click anywhere outside to collapse.
